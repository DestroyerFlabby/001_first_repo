# AI Voice Guidelines

## Purpose

AI can help create hooks, captions, summaries, and optional voiceover, but the page needs a consistent human point of view.

## Tone Rules

- Use short sentences.
- Make the point clear in the first line.
- Avoid exaggerated claims.
- Avoid fake certainty.
- Avoid generic motivational language.
- Make every caption explain why the clip matters.

## Optional AI Voice

Use AI voice only when it adds transformation:

- Short intro context.
- A 1-sentence setup before the clip.
- A 1-sentence takeaway after the clip.
- A quick summary for clips where the original speaker is unclear.

Do not overuse AI voice if it makes the page feel automated.

## Text Overlay Style

Use text overlays to frame the clip:

- "Most people miss this part..."
- "This is why beginners fail at..."
- "The real lesson is not what you think."
- "Watch how he explains..."

## Consistency Checklist

- Same tone across posts.
- Similar caption structure.
- Repeated visual format.
- Clear niche.
- No random viral clips outside the page's theme.
