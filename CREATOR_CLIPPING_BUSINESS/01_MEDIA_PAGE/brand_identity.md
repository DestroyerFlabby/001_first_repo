# Brand Identity

## Positioning

A sharp, practical media page that turns long-form ideas into short, useful lessons.

## Audience

Ambitious creators, young professionals, entrepreneurs, students, and people interested in business, mindset, money, and execution.

## Tone

- Clear.
- Direct.
- Useful.
- Curious.
- Slightly opinionated.

Avoid sounding like a spam repost page or generic motivational account.

## Visual Style

- Clean subtitles.
- Strong first-frame hook.
- High contrast text.
- Consistent colors.
- Minimal clutter.
- Occasional B-roll or visual context when it improves understanding.

## Brand Promise

Useful ideas, compressed.

## Naming Ideas

- Founder Frames
- Signal Clips
- The Better Clip
- Sharp Takes Daily
- Build Signal
- Context Clips
