# Content Pillars

Use 3-5 pillars so the page feels focused but not repetitive.

## 1. Business Insights

Lessons from founders, operators, investors, and creators. Focus on useful ideas, not generic motivation.

Example angles:

- How a founder thinks about risk.
- Why a business model works.
- A mistake beginners make.

## 2. Mindset and Discipline

Clips about consistency, focus, patience, and execution. Avoid empty hype.

Example angles:

- The boring habit behind success.
- Why most people quit too early.
- How to stay consistent when results are slow.

## 3. Controversial Takes

Strong opinions that spark comments. Use balance and context so the page feels thoughtful.

Example angles:

- An unpopular business opinion.
- A creator disagreeing with common advice.
- A hot take with a practical lesson.

## 4. Lessons and Mistakes

Break down what viewers can learn from someone else's experience.

Example angles:

- What this founder learned the hard way.
- A mistake that cost time or money.
- What beginners should avoid.

## 5. Tools, Systems, and Execution

Practical workflows, systems, and tactics.

Example angles:

- How to structure your day.
- How creators repurpose content.
- How businesses use short-form content.
