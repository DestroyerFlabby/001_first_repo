# Monetization Paths

Do not rely only on platform payouts. Platform monetization can be slow, inconsistent, and strict about originality.

## 1. Brand Deals

Once the page has a clear niche and consistent engagement, sell sponsored placements to relevant brands.

Examples:

- AI tools.
- Creator tools.
- Finance apps.
- Education products.
- Productivity tools.

## 2. Affiliate Links

Promote tools that match the audience.

Examples:

- Editing software.
- AI caption tools.
- Newsletter platforms.
- Course platforms.

## 3. Selling Services

Use the media page as proof that you understand short-form content. Add a simple CTA in the bio:

"Need clips like this? DM 'clips'."

## 4. Lead Generation

Build a page around a niche, then direct attention into another offer.

Examples:

- Clipping service.
- Consulting.
- Digital product.
- Newsletter.

## 5. Platform Monetization

YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram may pay creators, but eligibility and payout quality vary. YouTube especially requires originality, consistency, and transformed content.

Treat platform payouts as upside, not the core business model.
