# Post Templates

## 1. The Lesson Clip

Hook: "This is the part most people skip."

Clip: Speaker explains the idea.

Takeaway: Add a caption explaining the lesson in plain language.

## 2. The Mistake Clip

Hook: "This mistake costs beginners months."

Clip: Speaker describes the mistake or consequence.

Takeaway: Explain what the viewer should do instead.

## 3. The Contrarian Take

Hook: "This sounds wrong until you think about it."

Clip: Speaker shares a strong opinion.

Takeaway: Add balance in the caption. Explain when the take applies.

## 4. The Founder Breakdown

Hook: "Watch how this founder thinks about risk."

Clip: Founder explains a decision.

Takeaway: Turn the clip into a business lesson.

## 5. The Before-You-Start Clip

Hook: "Before you start doing this, understand one thing."

Clip: Speaker gives beginner advice.

Takeaway: Add a checklist or simple next step in the caption.
