# Media Page Strategy

## Goal

Build a semi-faceless curated media brand around a clear niche. The page should feel like a source of useful, repeatable insight, not a random repost account.

## Core Rule

Do not simply repost clips with subtitles. Each post must be transformed.

Transformation can include:

- A stronger opening hook.
- Added context before or after the clip.
- On-screen framing that explains why the moment matters.
- A written takeaway in the caption.
- Pattern recognition across multiple creators or clips.
- Visual edits, B-roll, diagrams, headlines, or summaries.

## Positioning Ideas

- Business lessons from founders.
- Money, career, and wealth mindset.
- Creator economy insights.
- Sales, persuasion, and communication clips.
- Fitness discipline and performance mindset.
- AI, technology, and future-of-work clips.

## Publishing Strategy

Post daily if possible. Start with volume, then improve based on data. The first goal is to learn which hooks, topics, and creators get saves, shares, comments, and follows.

## Quality Bar

Every clip should answer one question:

Why should someone watch this instead of the original?
