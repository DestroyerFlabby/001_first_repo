# Clipping Service Offer

## Simple Offer

I turn your long-form content into short-form clips designed for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

## What Is Included

- Review long-form videos, podcasts, webinars, or streams.
- Select the strongest moments.
- Create a hook and framing angle.
- Edit clips with subtitles, pacing, and basic visuals.
- Write captions and hashtags.
- Deliver ready-to-post files.
- Track performance if the client shares results.

## Who It Helps

- Creators.
- Coaches.
- Founders.
- Podcasters.
- Consultants.
- Fitness professionals.
- Educators.

## Main Promise

Save time and turn existing content into consistent short-form posts.

## Important Positioning

This is not just cutting clips. The service includes clip selection, hook writing, framing, and transformation so the short-form content can stand on its own.
