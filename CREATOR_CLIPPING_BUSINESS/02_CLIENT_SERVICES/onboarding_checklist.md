# Client Onboarding Checklist

## Basic Info

- Client name.
- Brand name.
- Main platforms.
- Target audience.
- Niche.
- Main business goal.

## Content Access

- YouTube channel link.
- Podcast link.
- Google Drive or Dropbox folder.
- Raw video access if needed.
- Brand assets.
- Existing best-performing clips.

## Style Preferences

- Subtitle style.
- Font preferences.
- Brand colors.
- Example clips they like.
- Example clips they dislike.
- Tone: professional, funny, bold, calm, educational.

## Delivery Details

- Number of clips.
- Clip length preference.
- Platforms needed.
- File format.
- Delivery deadline.
- Revision policy.

## Performance Tracking

Ask whether they can share:

- Views.
- Likes.
- Comments.
- Shares.
- Saves.
- Follows gained.
- Leads or booked calls.
