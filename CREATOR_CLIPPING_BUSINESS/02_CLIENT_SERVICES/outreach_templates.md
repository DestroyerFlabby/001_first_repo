# Outreach Templates

Keep messages short. Make it obvious you watched their content.

## Template 1: Free Sample

Hey [Name], I watched your video on [topic]. There were a few moments that would work well as Shorts/Reels. I can make one sample clip for you so you can see the style. Want me to send it over?

## Template 2: Specific Moment

Hey [Name], the part in your recent video where you talked about [specific moment] would make a strong short-form clip. I help creators turn long-form content into clips with hooks, captions, and clean subtitles. Want me to cut that moment for you?

## Template 3: Time-Saver

Hey [Name], noticed you post long-form content but not many short clips. I can help turn each video into multiple Reels/Shorts so you get more mileage from content you already made. Open to a small test batch?

## Template 4: Portfolio Angle

Hey [Name], I am building a clipping service for creators in [niche]. I would love to make 2-3 clips from one of your videos at a starter rate. If you like them, we can talk monthly.

## Template 5: Results Follow-Up

Hey [Name], following up. I still think your content has strong short-form potential, especially around [topic]. Happy to send one sample clip so you can judge the quality with no pressure.
