# Pricing

Start simple. Raise prices when you have proof, speed, and a repeatable delivery process.

## Per Clip

- Starter: $25-$50 per clip.
- Better positioning: $75-$150 per clip.
- Premium editing: $150+ per clip.

Use per-clip pricing for new clients or test projects.

## Monthly Packages

### Starter

- 8 clips per month.
- Basic captions and subtitles.
- Best for testing consistency.

### Growth

- 16 clips per month.
- Hooks, captions, subtitles, and performance review.
- Best for active creators.

### Authority

- 30 clips per month.
- Full short-form repurposing support.
- Best for creators posting daily.

## Performance-Based

Use only after trust is built.

Options:

- Base retainer plus bonus for clips above a view threshold.
- Lower monthly fee plus revenue share.
- Bonus for booked calls, leads, or sales if tracking is reliable.

## Recommendation

Start with a small paid test package:

3 clips for a fixed price. If they like the work, move to a monthly package.
