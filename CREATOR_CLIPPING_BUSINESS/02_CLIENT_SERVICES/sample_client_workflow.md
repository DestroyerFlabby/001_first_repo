# Sample Client Workflow

## 1. Discovery

Understand the client's niche, goals, audience, and current content.

## 2. Content Access

Get long-form videos, podcast links, raw files, or channel access.

## 3. Clip Selection

Review the content and identify strong moments based on:

- Clear lesson.
- Emotional reaction.
- Controversial point.
- Useful explanation.
- Strong story.

## 4. Hook and Framing

Write the opening hook and decide the angle of the clip.

## 5. Editing

Add subtitles, pacing, cuts, basic visual enhancements, and export in the right format.

## 6. Caption and Hashtags

Write platform-friendly captions and a few relevant hashtags.

## 7. Client Review

Send clips for review. Keep revisions simple and specific.

## 8. Delivery

Deliver final files with captions in a folder or tracker.

## 9. Performance Review

Ask for performance results after posting. Use winners to guide future clip selection.
