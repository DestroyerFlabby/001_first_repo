# Posting Workflow

## Manual Posting Flow

1. Export final video in 9:16 format.
2. Save caption and hashtags.
3. Upload natively to each platform.
4. Check thumbnail or first frame.
5. Add caption.
6. Add relevant hashtags.
7. Post or schedule.
8. Record the post in the content calendar.
9. Check performance after 24 hours, 72 hours, and 7 days.

## TikTok

Manual posting is usually the safest workflow. TikTok automation can be limited, unreliable, or restricted depending on account access and tool support.

Best realistic workflow:

- Prepare clips and captions in batches.
- Save drafts manually in TikTok.
- Post manually at planned times.
- Track results in the performance CSV.

## Instagram Reels

Options:

- Manual posting through the Instagram app.
- Meta Business Suite scheduling.
- Third-party schedulers where supported.

## YouTube Shorts

Options:

- Manual upload through YouTube Studio.
- Schedule Shorts in YouTube Studio.
- Use consistent titles and descriptions.

## Scheduling Tools

- Metricool.
- Buffer.
- Hootsuite.
- Later.
- Meta Business Suite.

## Recommended Beginner Workflow

Batch edit 7-14 clips at a time, schedule what can be scheduled, and manually post TikTok if automation creates friction.
