# 02 Intermediate

Pipeline step: clip transformation.

This zone turns raw material into useful clip ideas.

Use it for:

- Transcripts.
- Clip candidate notes.
- Hook ideas.
- Framing angles.
- Rights review notes.

The goal is to decide what the clip is about and how it becomes original enough to post.

## Subfolders

`TRANSCRIPTS`

Raw or cleaned transcripts.

`CLIP_CANDIDATES`

Candidate moments, timestamps, hooks, and transformation notes.
