# B-Roll Ideas

B-roll should clarify or emphasize the point. Do not add random visuals just to fill space.

## Business Clips

- Founder working.
- Laptop screen.
- Team meeting.
- Product screenshot.
- Revenue chart.
- Customer reviews.

## Money and Career Clips

- Calendar.
- Spreadsheet.
- Office desk.
- City footage.
- Notes or planning board.

## Creator Economy Clips

- Editing timeline.
- Phone recording setup.
- YouTube analytics.
- Social media dashboard.
- Content calendar.

## Mindset Clips

- Early morning routine.
- Gym footage.
- Notebook.
- Walking shot.
- Quiet work session.

## Educational Clips

- Simple diagram.
- Screenshot of key term.
- Step-by-step checklist.
- Before/after process.
