# Subtitle Style Guide

## Basic Style

- Use large, readable text.
- Keep subtitles centered in the safe viewing area.
- Avoid covering faces.
- Use high contrast.
- Keep lines short.
- Do not overload the screen.

## Pacing

- Break subtitles by natural speech rhythm.
- Keep each subtitle on screen long enough to read.
- Remove filler words when possible.

## Highlighting

Highlight only the most important words.

Good highlight targets:

- Numbers.
- Strong verbs.
- Emotional words.
- Key nouns.
- Contrasts.

## Fonts

Use clean sans-serif fonts:

- Inter.
- Montserrat.
- Arial.
- Helvetica.

## Consistency

Use the same style across clips for the same page or client.
