# Clip Selection Prompt

Use this with a transcript or long-form video notes.

```text
You are a short-form content strategist.

Analyze this transcript and identify the best moments for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

For each clip idea, return:
- timestamp or transcript section
- suggested clip title
- why this moment could perform well
- emotional trigger
- target audience
- suggested hook text
- suggested length
- transformation idea to make it original

Prioritize moments with:
- clear lessons
- strong opinions
- useful advice
- emotional stories
- surprising insights
- conflict or tension

Transcript:
[paste transcript here]
```
