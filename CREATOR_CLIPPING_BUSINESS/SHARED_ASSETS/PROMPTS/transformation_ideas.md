# Transformation Ideas

Use these to make clips more original and valuable.

## Hook Text

Add a strong opening line that frames the clip before the viewer hears the speaker.

Examples:

- "This is why most beginners quit."
- "He explains the mistake nobody talks about."
- "This changed how I think about money."

## Commentary

Add a short intro, outro, or caption that explains the lesson.

## Storytelling Framing

Turn a random moment into a clear story:

- Problem.
- Tension.
- Insight.
- Takeaway.

## Summarization

Add a final line that summarizes the point in plain language.

## Added Context

Explain who the speaker is, what situation they are talking about, or why the moment matters.

## Visual Enhancement

Use charts, B-roll, screenshots, headlines, or examples to make the idea easier to understand.

## Pattern Recognition

Connect the clip to a broader lesson:

- "This is a common founder mistake."
- "This is how top creators think about distribution."
- "This applies to anyone trying to build online."
