# Cover Letter Template Notes

Source template:

- `Nisarg Dave - Cover Letter - Sentry.docx`

Use this cover letter as the base for tailored applications. Keep the contact header, Dawson Partners experience paragraph, stakeholder communication paragraph, closing, and signature mostly unchanged unless the target job clearly needs different emphasis.

## Fields To Customize

For each new application, update:

- Company name: replace `Sentry`
- Role title: replace `Technical Customer Success Manager`
- Greeting: use `Dear Hiring Team,` unless a recruiter/hiring manager name is known
- Opening paragraph: rewrite the first 2-4 sentences to connect Nisarg's background to the specific company, product, and role
- Company-specific paragraph: update the mission/product line near the end so it does not sound copied from another company

## Reusable Positioning

The strongest recurring themes in this template are:

- Technical + business bridge
- Python, SQL, Google Cloud, data pipelines, analytics, and automation
- Debugging, monitoring, reliability, and process improvement
- Translating technical work into clear outcomes for stakeholders
- Customer-facing communication and cross-functional delivery

## Suggested File Naming

Save generated cover letters as:

`Nisarg Dave - Cover Letter - COMPANY - ROLE.docx`

Examples:

- `Nisarg Dave - Cover Letter - Sentry - Technical Customer Success Manager.docx`
- `Nisarg Dave - Cover Letter - Stripe - Solutions Engineer.docx`

## Tailoring Prompt

When tailoring this letter, use this instruction:

> Tailor the cover letter in `Nisarg Dave - Cover Letter - Sentry.docx` for `[ROLE_TITLE]` at `[COMPANY_NAME]`. Keep the reusable Dawson Partners and communication paragraphs mostly intact. Change the company name, role title, opening paragraph, and company-specific mission/product lines. Keep the tone professional, concise, and direct.
