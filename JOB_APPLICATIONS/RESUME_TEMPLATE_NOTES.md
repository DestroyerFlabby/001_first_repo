## Intended Use

Use the two resumes as starting points for role-specific versions:

- Product / project / program management roles
- Technical, data, automation, customer success, solutions, or pivot roles

## Tailoring Checklist

For each job application, choose the closest base resume and adjust:

- Target title / summary
- Top skills and keywords from the job description
- Bullet order and emphasis
- Tools and technologies mentioned by the employer
- Metrics, business impact, and stakeholder-facing work

## Suggested File Naming

Save tailored resumes as:

`Nisarg Dave - Resume - COMPANY - ROLE.pdf`

Examples:

- `Nisarg Dave - Resume - Sentry - Technical Customer Success Manager.pdf`
- `Nisarg Dave - Resume - Stripe - Solutions Engineer.pdf`

## Tailoring Prompt

When tailoring a resume, use this instruction:

> Use the closest resume PDF in `JOB_APPLICATIONS` as the base for `[ROLE_TITLE]` at `[COMPANY_NAME]`. Prioritize relevant experience, skills, and keywords from the job description. Keep the resume truthful and ATS-friendly, and do not invent companies, titles, credentials, tools, or metrics.
