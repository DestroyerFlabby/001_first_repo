## Bottleneck Notes

The category tracks leading-edge logic fabrication capacity and the geographic
concentration risk around advanced-node manufacturing. TSMC is the purest
public exposure in this set. Samsung Foundry and Intel Foundry are represented
through their listed parents, so their portfolio returns also reflect the
parents' other businesses.

Rapidus and imec remain visible in the research map because they matter to the
longer-term process-development landscape even though they cannot be recorded
as direct public-equity positions.

`SMSN.IL` is Samsung Electronics' USD-denominated London GDR.
