## Bottleneck Notes

ASML is the only commercial EUV lithography-system producer. The concentration
extends into key components: ZEISS SMT supplies EUV optics, TRUMPF supplies
high-power laser technology used to generate EUV light, and Cymer is ASML's
light-source subsidiary. Canon and Nikon provide public exposure to alternative
and mature-node lithography paths rather than direct EUV competition.

The Canon and Nikon positions use USD-quoted OTC ADRs. Their liquidity is lower
than the primary Japanese listings, so their tracker prices should be treated
as portfolio estimates rather than assumptions about executable institutional
fills.
