## Bottleneck Notes

- Standard DRAM and NAND supply is concentrated among a small number of vendors.
- HBM supply is more concentrated near term and is a critical input for AI accelerators.
- Memory demand and inventory swings can produce sharp pricing cycles.
- 3D NAND layer-count scaling depends on deep-etch capability concentrated among a limited group of equipment suppliers.
