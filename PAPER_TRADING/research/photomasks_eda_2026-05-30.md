## Bottleneck Notes

The category tracks two different bottlenecks:

- EDA software is concentrated among Synopsys, Cadence, and Siemens EDA.
- Physical mask production depends on pellicles, photomask blanks, e-beam mask
  writers, and defect inspection tools.

NuFlare and IMS remain in the research map even though they do not have direct
public listings. This avoids overstating exposure by substituting broad parent
companies whose other businesses dominate their valuation.

The Siemens position is intentionally different: `SIEGY` is included because
Siemens EDA is one of the core named EDA platforms, but its tracker result still
represents Siemens AG as a whole rather than a pure-play EDA return.
