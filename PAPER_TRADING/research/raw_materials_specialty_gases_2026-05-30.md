# Raw Materials and Specialty Gases

Research snapshot generated: 2026-05-30

## Bottleneck Thesis

Semiconductor manufacturing depends on concentrated upstream inputs:

- ultra-pure neon, krypton, and xenon for lithography-related processes
- rare-earth elements and strategic metals
- high-purity quartz used in semiconductor manufacturing
- cobalt, germanium, gallium, hafnium, and tungsten exposure
- specialty gases and related process materials

This portfolio is a category tracker, not a buy recommendation.
